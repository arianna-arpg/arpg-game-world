Passive Tree Builder

A full interactive build planner on Hollow Wake's real 233-node tree — allocate from your class start, watch stats total live, and share a build with a link.

What's landing here

  • The real tree, rendered from each node's authored x/y and links — pan, zoom, and click to allocate.
  • Connectivity enforced from your class start; keystones and notables called out.
  • A live stat summary that adds up every allocated node's modifiers.
  • Shareable build URLs — send someone your exact allocation.

The passive data is already exported to data/passives.json by the same pipeline that feeds the Database — so the builder plugs straight in and stays at parity with the game.
